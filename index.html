Research Projects

GPU Forward Progress

CPU threads are guaranteed to make forward progress eventually, but many GPUs don't have this guarantee. This makes it much easier to introduce bugs or deadlocks when communicating between threads on a GPU. I'm working with Prof. Tyler Sorensen to investigate this behavior in consumer GPUs.

CPU Frontend Microarchitecture

Datacenter CPUs have very deep and wide pipelines. They rely on branch predictions to overcome control hazards and keep the pipeline full. While modern branch predictors are highly accurate, bad speculation is still an issue, especially for larger applications. I'm working with Prof. Heiner Litz to reduce the side effects, including power and cache pollution, of bad speculation in data-center CPUs.
